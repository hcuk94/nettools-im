UK Phone Numbers

        /\+44\s\d{2}\s\d{4}\s\d{4}|0\d{4}\s\d{6}/g
        
Call our London office on +44 20 7946 0958 during business hours.

        Match: +44 20 7946 0958

The meeting is scheduled for 25/12/2024 in the conference room.

        Match: 25/12/2024
